# ShortPurify Marketing Strategy Document

---

## 1. Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

**Primary:** Solo content creators (YouTubers, podcasters, educators) with 5K–500K subscribers who publish long-form video weekly and struggle to maintain a short-form presence alongside it.

**Secondary:** Social media managers at small agencies or brands managing 3–10 client accounts who need to repurpose video at scale without hiring editors.

**Firmographic signals:**
- Posts on YouTube at least 2x/month
- Active on TikTok or YouTube Shorts but inconsistently
- Uses tools like CapCut, Canva, or Descript today
- Team size: 1–5 people
- Budget: $20–$150/month for creator tools

**Pain points:** Editing short clips manually takes 2–4 hours per video, posting to multiple platforms requires switching between apps, and they lack confidence knowing which moments will actually perform.

---

## 2. Positioning Statement

**For** content creators and social media managers **who** waste hours manually clipping and editing long videos, **ShortPurify** is the AI-powered repurposing platform **that** automatically finds your best moments, scores them for virality, and publishes them across TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and Bluesky in under five minutes — **unlike** OpusClip or Munch, which require significant manual review and don't offer native Bluesky publishing.

---

## 3. Messaging Framework

### Hero Message
*"Paste a link. Go viral. Save 10 hours a week."*

### Value Pillars

| Pillar | Message | Proof Point |
|---|---|---|
| **Speed** | From 60-minute video to 4–6 clips in under 5 minutes | No editing skills required |
| **Confidence** | Know exactly which clip to post first | AI Virality Score 0–100 with reasoning |
| **Reach** | One upload, every platform | TikTok + YouTube Shorts + Bluesky simultaneously |
| **Control** | Your style, your captions | Subtitle burn-in with custom fonts and colors |

### Tone of Voice
Direct, energetic, and creator-native. Avoid corporate language. Write like a creator talking to another creator.

### Objection Handling
- *"I already use OpusClip"* → ShortPurify adds Bluesky publishing, virality scoring with AI reasoning, and team workspaces competitors lack.
- *"I can just do it manually"* → You're trading 10 hours a week for $0 saved. That's not a strategy.

## 4. Channel Prioritization Report

### Ranked Highest to Lowest Priority

**1. TikTok & YouTube Shorts (Organic Content) — Priority: Critical**
ShortPurify's output *is* the product demo. Publish clips made with ShortPurify showing the tool in action. This is zero-cost, self-reinforcing proof that the product works. Target creator and marketing hashtags. Post 5x/week minimum.

**2. Twitter/X Creator Communities — Priority: High**
Creators and social media managers congregate here. Share before/after clips, virality score screenshots, and time-saved stats. Engage in threads about content strategy. One viral tweet can drive hundreds of signups.

**3. YouTube (Tutorial & Demo Content) — Priority: High**
Publish a 2–3 minute demo video and short tutorials ("How I repurposed a 1-hour podcast in 5 minutes"). Ranks in search for terms like "repurpose YouTube videos automatically." Compounds over time with low ongoing effort.

**4. Reddit (r/NewTubers, r/socialmedia, r/ContentCreators) — Priority: Medium**
Highly targeted, high-intent audience. Share genuine value posts and answer questions where ShortPurify solves the problem. Avoid overt promotion — lead with helpfulness.

**5. Paid Ads (Meta/TikTok) — Priority: Low (defer to Day 30+)**
Too early to invest in paid until organic messaging is validated and conversion rate on the free trial is understood. Revisit after Week 4 with data.

## 5. 30-Day Roadmap

### Week 1 — Foundation (Days 1–7)
- [ ] Set up TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and X accounts under the ShortPurify brand
- [ ] Record a 2-minute screen-capture demo video using the actual product
- [ ] Publish the demo to YouTube and clip it into 3 shorts using ShortPurify itself
- [ ] Write 5 X posts: one product hook, one pain-point post, one "10 hours saved" stat post, one feature spotlight, one question to the audience
- [ ] Identify 10 Reddit threads where repurposing pain is discussed — comment helpfully on 5

### Week 2 — Content Engine (Days 8–14)
- [ ] Publish 5 TikTok/Shorts clips (mix of product demos and creator tips)
- [ ] Post daily on X; engage with 3 creator accounts per day
- [ ] Reach out to 5 micro-influencers (10K–100K YouTube subscribers) offering free Pro access in exchange for an honest mention or clip
- [ ] Write and publish one Reddit post in r/NewTubers: *"I built a tool that clips my podcast automatically — here's what I learned"*
- [ ] A/B test two homepage headline variants using a simple tool like Microsoft Clarity heatmaps

### Week 3 — Amplification (Days 15–21)
- [ ] Follow up with influencer outreach; aim for 2 confirmed partnerships
- [ ] Repurpose any high-performing X posts into TikTok talking-head clips
- [ ] Launch a simple referral hook: *"Tag a creator who needs this"* post on TikTok and X
- [ ] Post a YouTube tutorial: *"How to turn a 1-hour video into 5 viral clips (free tool)"*
- [ ] Collect 3 user testimonials via email or DM; add to homepage

### Week 4 — Optimize & Scale (Days 22–30)
- [ ] Review analytics: which clips, posts, and channels drove the most signups
- [ ] Double down on the top-performing content format with 3 new pieces
- [ ] Publish a case study post on X/Reddit: real numbers from a real user
- [ ] Set up a simple email welcome sequence (3 emails) for new free-trial signups
- [ ] Define paid ad creative brief based on the organic content that performed best — ready to launch in Month 2
