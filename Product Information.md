# Product Information

## Overview
**Product Name:** ShortPurify

**Website:** https://shortpurify.com/

**One-liner:** ShortPurify turns long videos into viral-ready short clips using AI, then publishes them directly to TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and Bluesky.

## What It Does
Users paste a YouTube link or upload a video file, and ShortPurify's AI automatically identifies the most engaging moments, generates captions, smart-crops for mobile, and scores each clip for virality. The built-in editor lets users tweak subtitles before publishing directly to connected social accounts or scheduling posts in advance. The whole process takes under five minutes for a 60-minute video.

## Key Features
1. Viral Hook Detection: AI scans the full video to identify moments with the highest retention and virality potential
2. AI Virality Scoring: every clip receives a 0–100 score with an AI-generated reason explaining why it will perform
3. Subtitle Editor with Burn-in: custom fonts, colors, and highlights burned permanently into exported clips
4. YouTube URL Import: paste any YouTube link and ShortPurify fetches, transcribes, and clips the video automatically
5. One-Click Multi-Platform Publishing: post simultaneously to YouTube Shorts, TikTok, and Bluesky from a single Publish Hub
6. Smart Scheduling: queue clips days or weeks in advance across all connected accounts
7. Team Collaboration: invite editors and managers to shared workspaces with role-based access control
